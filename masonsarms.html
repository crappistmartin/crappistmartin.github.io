Protest against the Loss of the Masons Arms Public House on East Street SE17


 
Please come along if you can to a brief protest outside the Masons Arms this Monday morning 14th Sept. We will be meeting to voice our dismay at the proposed loss of another Walworth pub and its historic facade and to get some photos for use in the local press. We will meet for 10 mins max at 8.30am on Monday 14th September outside the Masons Arms (109 East St, London SE17 2SB).

If you have a few minutes to write an objection to the planning application, then how to do it and some grounds to use are set out at:
http://walworthsociety.co.uk/index.php/news/153-masons-arms-planning-application-15-ap-3022

Thanks as always for all your support!
